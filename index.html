Introduction

Health and fitness are essential aspects of leading a balanced and happy life. From eating nutritious food to maintaining an active lifestyle, every small step contributes to overall well-being.

Top Fitness Trends

1. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) for quick and effective workouts.
2. Wearable fitness technology to track progress.
3. Mind-body exercises like yoga and pilates.
